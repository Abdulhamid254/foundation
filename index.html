STARBUCKS

We are hostel dedicated to provide reliable services of accomodation to students.The hostel was founded in 2019.

Among our main objectives is to give students friendly environment to students so that they can feel at home.We also offer a secure place for students to stay and also the right atmosphere for study and interchange of thoughts and ideas.

We offer the following services to the students:

  1. Food.
  2. Well furbished rooms.
  3. Study area.

FOOD

We provide breakfast and dinner to students for five days a week.
On weekends we provide breakfast,lunch and dinner.

Our food is both nutritious and tasty so that students would like it and it provide them with energy to continue with their studies efficiently.
The food is usually server in the food hall where all the students sit together and have their food together.
